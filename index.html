Hideo Kojima

Japanese video game designer, screenwriter, director, and producer.

Picture of Hideo Kojima on cover of issue number 65 of Official PlayStation Magazine UK

Kojima is the creator, director, and writer of a number of video games, most notably in the Metal Gear series of stealth games, as well as the adventure games Snatcher and Policenauts. He has also directed or produced games in other series, including Zone of the Enders, Boktai, and Castlevania: Lords of Shadow.
